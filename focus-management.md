# Focus Management

## Question 1: What is Deep Work?

Deep Work means working with full focus.

Deep Works means:

- No phone
- No social media

And it helps you to:

- Learn things faster
- Do better and hard work

## Question 2: How to Do Deep Work Properly?

- Make a fix time for work
- Work in a silent place
- Keep phone away from yourself
- Avoid social media usage

## Question 3: How to Use in Daily Life?

- Keep mobile away from you
- Take breaks between your work
- Go for a small walk

## Question 4: Dangers of Social Media

- It makes you addicted
- Wastage of time
- Makes you less concentrated
