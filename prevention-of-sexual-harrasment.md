# Prevention of Sexual Harrasment

## What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

- Sexual jokes, asking someone out repeatedly, or bad language about sex.
​
- Sharing porn.
​
- Unwanted touching or bad touch
​
- Spreading rumor about their sexuality


## What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

* Within three months, submit a formal complaint.
* Seek help if problems is big and also escalate.
* Assist the victim and provide testimony.
