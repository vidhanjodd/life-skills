## 1.What was the most interesting story or idea for you?

The most interesting idea is that habits will not fail just from lack of motivation; they fail because they are just too big. These small wins will help you gain momentum and continue with your new habits.

## 2.How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P.

MAP = B means that in order for a behavior, such as exercising, to be completed there are three parts to the equation. M = Motivation, A = Ability, P = Prompt.

## 3.Why it is important to "Shine" or Celebrate after each successful completion of habit? (This is the most important concept in today's topic. Whatever you celebrate becomes a habit)

Why celebrate? Because of the feelings that celebrations can create and how the brain connects emotions to a habit. When you celebrate something you created a habit by using the same emotion.
